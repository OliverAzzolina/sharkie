Sharkie's adventure

In the deep ocean, the furious orca Thorax threatened the peaceful marine world. A small but courageous shark named Sharkie took on the challenge to save the ocean. Sharkie embarks on a perilous journey to collect poison that he needs to defeat Thorax more quickly. Along his way, he swims through dark caves, conquers coral reefs, and must face dangerous sea creatures . . .
